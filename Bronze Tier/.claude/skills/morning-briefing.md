# Morning Briefing — Agent Skill

You are the AI Employee. Generate a Monday Morning CEO Briefing (or daily briefing) for your owner.

## Your Task

1. **Read** these files:
   - `AI_Employee_Vault/Business_Goals.md`
   - `AI_Employee_Vault/Company_Handbook.md`
   - All files in `AI_Employee_Vault/Done/` (what was completed)
   - All files in `AI_Employee_Vault/Needs_Action/` (what's pending)
   - All files in `AI_Employee_Vault/Pending_Approval/` (awaiting action)
   - Recent logs from `AI_Employee_Vault/Logs/`

2. **Analyze** the data:
   - What was accomplished recently?
   - What is still pending?
   - Are there any bottlenecks or overdue items?
   - Any patterns worth noting?

3. **Write the briefing** to `AI_Employee_Vault/Briefings/` folder (create it if needed):
   - Filename: `<YYYY-MM-DD>_Morning_Briefing.md`

4. **Update Dashboard** with a note that briefing was generated

## Briefing Format

```markdown
# Morning CEO Briefing — <Date>
---
generated: <ISO timestamp>
---

## Executive Summary
<2-3 sentence overview of current state>

## Completed Recently
<bullet list of what's in /Done>

## Pending Items
<bullet list from /Needs_Action with priority>

## Needs Your Approval
<list from /Pending_Approval — be specific about what action is needed>

## Bottlenecks / Flags
<anything taking too long or unusual>

## Proactive Suggestions
<1-3 suggestions based on patterns you noticed>

---
*Generated by AI Employee v0.1 (Bronze Tier)*
```

## Rules
- Be concise but complete — busy executives read fast
- Highlight urgent items at the top
- Be specific about dollar amounts, deadlines, and names if available
- Always end with 1-3 actionable suggestions
